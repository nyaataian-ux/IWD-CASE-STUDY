The Brief

A celebration rooted in
health & sisterhood

Full event brand identity for Mega We Care Ghana in partnership with the Ministry of Health and the Medical Women's Association of Ghana (MWAG). The brief called for a visual language that honoured women's health, felt festive and alive, and unified three institutional partners under one cohesive voice.

Brand Motif

The butterfly — product & purpose

A butterfly motif anchored the visual system, simultaneously activating four Mega We Care health products — Fenza, ENAT 400, MaxCal, and Ginsomineve — while symbolising transformation, care, and feminine strength.
