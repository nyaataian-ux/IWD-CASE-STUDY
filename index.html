The Brief

A celebration rooted in health & sisterhood

Mega We Care Ghana wanted to mark International Women's Day with an event that did more than celebrate — it needed to activate its women's health product range (Fenza, ENAT 400, MaxCal, Ginsomineve) in a way that felt warm, empowering, and Ghanaian.

The event was co-hosted with the Ministry of Health Ghana and the Medical Women's Association of Ghana (MWAG), raising the stakes for the visual identity: it had to carry institutional weight while still feeling joyful and community-centred.
